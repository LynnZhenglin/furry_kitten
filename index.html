DEVELOP
Concept
I think a low-cost and more private design, also available anytime and anywhere, should be created to meet patients' psychological needs.
I decided to design a relaxing game for female depressed patients.

What is Game Therapy?
Gamification Design
Game therapy allows patients to present their true selves in a relaxed and egalitarian state and has a better intervention effect on social disoders.
I use scenario-based games as the mainline of treatment, integrated with targeted offline activities.
